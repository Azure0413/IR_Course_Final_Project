Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

Ingredients
1 cup strawberries

1 banana

½ cup yogurt

¼ cup pineapple juice

1 ½ teaspoons white sugar

1 teaspoon orange juice

1 teaspoon milk

Directions
Gather all ingredients.

Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender.

Blend until smooth.
