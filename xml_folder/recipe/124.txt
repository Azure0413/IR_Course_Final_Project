Easy Greek Yogurt Cucumber Sauce

Ingredients
1 cup plain yogurt

1 cup sour cream

1 small cucumber, peeled, seeded, and finely chopped

1 green onion

¼ cup crumbled feta cheese

1 garlic clove, minced

1 teaspoon white vinegar

½ teaspoon dried oregano

½ teaspoon lemon juice

¼ teaspoon minced lemon zest

salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions
Combine yogurt, sour cream, cucumber, green onion, feta cheese, garlic, vinegar, oregano, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and black pepper in a bowl; cover and chill 8 hours or overnight before serving.
