Wonderful Yogurt Cake

Ingredients
1 cup brown sugar

½ cup butter, softened

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 large egg

2 cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

½ teaspoon baking powder

1 cup plain yogurt

Directions
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9-inch Bundt pan.

Cream together brown sugar, butter, and vanilla in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in egg until smooth. Combine flour, baking soda, and baking powder; stir into the batter alternately with yogurt. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.

Bake in the preheated oven until a knife inserted into the crown comes out clean, about 50 minutes.
